# 🚀 Helix Hackathon Demo Script
**Total Time: 5:00**

## Setup & Prep (Before Demo)
- Ensure all 3 services are running (`bash start.sh`).
- Have browser open to `http://localhost:5173/`.
- Pre-fill the Personal Risk form on another tab (if needed) to save time typing.

---

## The Script

### **00:00 — Intro & Dashboard (The Intelligence Core)**
*Start on `/dashboard`.*
**"Welcome to Helix—a Predictive Biomedical & Public Health Intelligence Platform."**
- "We are transitioning public health from reactive to predictive."
- Show the **Hero KPI Cards**: Point out *Lives Protected* and *Platform Confidence*.
- Show the **Trend Charts**: "Here we see active case trajectories. But looking backwards isn't enough..."

### **00:45 — Outbreak Map (Spatial Awareness)**
*Navigate to `/outbreak-map` via Sidebar.*
- "This is our Live Threat Landscape."
- Toggle **Heatmap** and **Risk Zones**.
- Zoom into a specific region (e.g., Maharashtra) to show the pulsing critical zones.
- "We don't just track where cases are; we track where they *will be* based on environmental data and symptom clustering."

### **01:30 — Regional Detail & Prediction**
*Click on a city (e.g., Bangalore or Mumbai).*
- Show the slide-out regional detail panel.
- "Notice the predictive trajectory curve here. Our ensemble model (ARIMA + Prophet + LSTM) is predicting a spike in Dengue in 8 days due to recent humidity increases."

### **02:00 — The Trigger (End-to-End Alert Demo)**
*Navigate to `/alert-demo`.*
- "Let's see the autonomous engine in action. I'm going to simulate an emerging critical event."
- **CLICK TRIGGER SIMULATION**.
- "Watch the system ingest the anomaly, run it through the ML cluster, and escalate the severity."
- *Wait for the mockup email to pop up.*
- "In seconds, the appropriate health authority receives actionable intelligence."

### **02:45 — Alerts Management**
*Navigate to `/alerts`.*
- "That alert immediately hits our Command Center feed, complete with a recommended action plan (e.g., 'Pre-position 500 ORS kits')."

### **03:15 — Personal Risk (Digital Health Twin)**
*Navigate to `/personal-risk` and `/health-twin`.*
- "Helix isn't just for governments; it scales down to the individual."
- Show the **Digital Health Twin**.
- "This creates a longitudinal projection of a user's health. Watch what happens when we model a lifestyle change..."
- *Adjust the 'Weight Loss' or 'Smoking' slider.*
- "The risk curve dynamically flattens. We are democratizing preventative medicine."

### **04:15 — Validation & Trust**
*Navigate to `/validation`.*
- "In healthcare, trust is everything. Our Model Validation dashboard proves our efficacy transparently."
- Point out the 87% Ensemble Accuracy and the SHAP interpretability metrics.

### **04:30 — The Impact**
*Navigate to `/impact`.*
- "What does this mean in the real world?"
- Highlight the **1,847 Lives Protected** and **₹2.4 Crore Healthcare Costs Saved**.
- Show the *Lives Protected Zone* chart. "This gap between the red and green curves? That is the value of 8.3 days of early warning."

### **04:50 — The Vision & Architecture**
*Navigate to `/architecture` or `/roadmap`.*
- "Helix is built to scale. From this MVP today, to an automated Kubernetes cluster managing national ICMR integration in Phase 3."
- "We are Team AV26-020. Thank you."

---
*End of Demo.*
